FoodSaver- Keeps Track of your Food

Ever wonder tons of food gets wasted due negligence or lack of monitoring, Food Saver is here to save you

Food waste accounts for about 6% of worldwide greenhouse gas emissions. This results in environmental damage, global warming, food waste (which can be fed to the hungry), resource waste, and financial waste.  FoodSaver keeps track of the food you have in your home or restaurant and keeps reminding you to eat it up before it spoils and goes to waste.

  • Provide a platform for everyone to keep track of the food items.
  • Alerts user to use food item before it got expire.
  • All Info on our dedicated application

An upload feature where the user can upload their grocery receipt and feed items, and add the product along with the details automatically.
